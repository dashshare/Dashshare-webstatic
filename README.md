# Dashshare apk
Welcome to Dashshare APP, This is a **Android APP** to upload and share level in geometry dash. Here are the features in the app:
## 1. Disconver level in app
When open the app, you see disconver to explore yourself by click on the level. If level pages error, one option to redirect on broswer
## 2. Upload
You can upload upload level by click "+" to upload level. You can open "https://level.dashshare.pages.dev/create" to upload on web

Website: [Dashshare Web](https://dashshare.pages.dev)
